GIGA-42 Announces Major Milestone in Iceland Project Development

Significant progress made in environmental assessments and regulatory approvals for our flagship geothermal and wind energy project

GIGA-42 Communications Team 5 min read

REYKJAVIK, Iceland ‚Äì November 27, 2025 ‚Äì GIGA-42, a leading developer of large-scale renewable energy projects across Europe, today announced a significant milestone in the development of its flagship Iceland renewable energy project, marking substantial progress in environmental assessments and regulatory approvals for the ambitious 21 GW geothermal and wind energy initiative.

Environmental Assessment Milestone

The company has successfully completed the comprehensive Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) for Phase 1 of the Iceland project, covering the initial 5 GW capacity deployment across strategically selected sites in the Reykjanes Peninsula and northeastern Iceland. The assessment, conducted in collaboration with leading Icelandic environmental consultancies and international experts, demonstrates the project's commitment to maintaining Iceland's pristine natural environment while harnessing its abundant renewable energy resources.

Regulatory Approvals and Government Support

The Iceland project has received preliminary approval from the National Energy Authority (Orkustofnun) and strong support from the Icelandic government, recognizing the project's potential to position Iceland as a major renewable energy exporter to Europe. The regulatory framework includes provisions for:

  • Streamlined permitting processes for renewable energy infrastructure
  • Grid connection agreements with Landsnet, Iceland's transmission system operator
  • Environmental monitoring protocols ensuring biodiversity protection
  • Community engagement frameworks supporting local economic development

Project Specifications and Technology

The Iceland project leverages cutting-edge technology to maximize energy output while minimizing environmental impact. The initiative combines:

Geothermal Component

  • Capacity: 12 GW geothermal power generation
  • Technology: Enhanced Geothermal Systems (EGS)
  • Efficiency: 95%+ capacity factor
  • Innovation: Binary cycle power plants for maximum efficiency

Wind Component

  • Capacity: 9 GW wind power generation
  • Technology: Next-generation offshore wind turbines
  • Efficiency: 50%+ capacity factor
  • Innovation: Floating wind platforms for deeper waters

Economic Impact and Job Creation

The project is expected to create significant economic opportunities for Iceland, with an estimated investment of ‚Ç¨15 billion over the construction phase and the creation of approximately 8,000 direct and indirect jobs. Local communities will benefit from:

  • Construction jobs during the 7-year development phase
  • Permanent operational positions for project lifecycle management
  • Skills development programs in partnership with Icelandic universities
  • Local supply chain opportunities for Icelandic businesses

International Significance

Beyond its local impact, the Iceland project represents a cornerstone of Europe's renewable energy transition strategy. The project will contribute significantly to the European Union's goal of carbon neutrality by 2050, with potential energy exports via undersea cables to the UK and continental Europe.

Next Steps and Timeline

With the environmental assessment completed and preliminary regulatory approvals secured, GIGA-42 will now proceed with detailed engineering design and financial close. The company expects to begin construction on Phase 1 in Q2 2025, with the first turbines and geothermal plants coming online by 2027.

The project timeline includes:

  • Q1 2025: Final regulatory approvals and financial close
  • Q2-Q4 2025: Infrastructure development and construction commencement
  • 2026-2027: Phase 1 commissioning (5 GW capacity)
  • 2028-2030: Full project completion (21 GW total capacity)

About GIGA-42

GIGA-42 is a leading developer of large-scale renewable energy projects across Europe, specializing in gigawatt-scale wind, solar, and geothermal installations. The company combines cutting-edge technology with environmental stewardship to deliver transformative energy solutions that support the transition to a carbon-neutral future. With projects spanning Iceland and Sweden, GIGA-42 is committed to creating sustainable value for communities, investors, and the planet.
