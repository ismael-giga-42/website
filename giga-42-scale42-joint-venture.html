GIGA-42 Announces Strategic Joint Venture for Nordic Data Center Development

Partnership with Scale42 combines renewable energy expertise with cutting-edge data center innovation to deliver sustainable AI infrastructure across Nordic markets

GIGA-42 Executive Team 3 min read

We are excited to announce a groundbreaking partnership that will reshape the landscape of sustainable data center development across the Nordic region. GIGA-42 Holdings Limited, our strategic joint venture with Scale42, represents the convergence of renewable energy innovation and next-generation computing infrastructure.

At GIGA-42, we've always believed that the future of computing must be sustainable. Our partnership with Scale42 embodies this vision by combining our deep expertise in renewable energy development with Scale42's proven track record in data center innovation. Why the Nordic Region?

The Nordic countries offer unparalleled advantages for sustainable data center development:

  • Abundant Renewable Energy: Access to geothermal, hydroelectric, and wind resources
  • Natural Cooling: Arctic climate reduces cooling costs and environmental impact
  • Political Stability: Stable regulatory environment and business-friendly policies
  • Digital Leadership: Progressive approach to digital infrastructure and AI development
  • Strategic Location: Optimal positioning for serving European and global markets
