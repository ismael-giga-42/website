GIGA-42 Announces Strategic Joint Venture for Nordic Data Center Development

Partnership with Scale42 combines renewable energy expertise with cutting-edge data center innovation to deliver sustainable AI infrastructure across Nordic markets

GIGA-42 Executive Team 3 min read

We are excited to announce the formation of GIGA-42, a groundbreaking joint venture between Scale42 and Global Interconnection Group (GIG). This partnership will reshape the landscape of sustainable data center development across the Nordic region, representing the convergence of Scale42's data center expertise with GIG's renewable energy innovation and next-generation computing infrastructure.

The Vision Behind the Partnership

GIGA-42 brings together two exceptional organizations with complementary strengths: Scale42's proven track record in data center innovation and Global Interconnection Group's deep expertise in renewable energy development. This joint venture embodies our shared vision that the future of computing must be sustainable. Together, we're not just building data centers ‚Äì we're creating an ecosystem where AI and machine learning can thrive while maintaining the highest environmental standards.

By integrating renewable energy generation with AI-optimized data centers, we're creating solutions that are both economically viable and environmentally responsible. The Nordic countries offer unparalleled advantages for sustainable data center development:

  • Abundant Renewable Energy: Access to geothermal, hydroelectric, and wind resources
  • Natural Cooling: Arctic climate reduces cooling costs and environmental impact
  • Political Stability: Stable regulatory environment and business-friendly policies
  • Digital Leadership: Progressive approach to digital infrastructure and AI development
  • Strategic Location: Optimal positioning for serving European and global markets

Our Joint Venture Partners

Scale42: Data Center Expertise

Scale42 is the data center development specialist in the GIGA-42 joint venture. Founded by the visionary team behind Fjord Power and Hydrokraft, Scale42 has deep roots in the Nordic energy sector and a proven track record of successful data center development. Their understanding of both the technical and regulatory landscape in Nordic markets is essential to GIGA-42's ambitious vision.

Key strengths that Scale42 brings to GIGA-42:

  • Proven data center development and operational expertise
  • Strong relationships with local governments and communities
  • Deep understanding of AI infrastructure requirements
  • Advanced technical capabilities and infrastructure knowledge
  • Track record of successful project delivery in Nordic markets

Global Interconnection Group: Energy and Infrastructure Foundation

Global Interconnection Group (GIG) is the energy and infrastructure partner in the GIGA-42 joint venture. GIG is an integrated platform to service, supply and invest in interconnector cables and wider energy transmission infrastructure projects. As an EuroNext-listed company, GIG brings unparalleled expertise from landmark projects including the groundbreaking Atlantic SuperConnection between Iceland and the UK, providing GIGA-42 with exceptional capabilities in large-scale energy infrastructure development.

Key strengths that GIG brings to GIGA-42:

  • 1,794MW interconnector expertise for renewable energy transmission
  • Deep understanding of Nordic energy markets and regulations
  • EuroNext listing and financial strength
  • Proven operational experience with major energy projects
  • Commitment to environmental sustainability and ESG principles
