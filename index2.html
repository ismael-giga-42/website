Our Mission

Integrated Digital Infrastructure

GIGA-42 is building digital infrastructure powering the sustainable AI movement. We bring together visionary partners, renewable power, cutting-edge infrastructure, and a long-term mindset.

GIGA-42 is a joint venture between Scale42, a developer and operator of AI data centres and Global Interconnection Group, an electrical and digital transmission infrastructure platform. Our mission is to meet the world's growing demand for secure, efficient digital capacity while maintaining our commitment to 100% renewable energy and environmental responsibility.

Our Nordic Platform

GIGA-42 is built on three pillars that define the core of our platform and competitive advantage

Integrated Energy & AI Infrastructure

We combine cutting-edge artificial intelligence capabilities with Nordic energy expertise to create intelligent, responsive data centers. Our infrastructure optimizes energy allocation, cooling, and computational resources in real-time, delivering superior efficiency and performance.

Powered by Nordic Renewables

The Nordic region offers unparalleled access to renewable energy sources including hydroelectric, geothermal, and wind power. Our commitment to 100% renewable energy ensures sustainability, reduces operational costs, and positions us as a leader in green technology infrastructure.

Scalable Platform Model

Our modular design and proven operational framework enable rapid, cost-effective expansion across the Nordic region and beyond. We deliver enterprise-grade data center solutions that grow with our partners' needs while maintaining exceptional reliability and performance standards.
