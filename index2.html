Our Mission

Integrated Digital Infrastructure

GIGA-42 is building digital infrastructure powering the sustainable AI movement. We bring together visionary partners, renewable power, cutting-edge infrastructure, and a long-term mindset.

GIGA-42 is a joint venture between Scale42, a developer and operator of AI data centres and Global Interconnection Group, an electrical and digital transmission infrastructure platform. Our mission is to meet the world's growing demand for secure, efficient digital capacity while maintaining our commitment to 100% renewable energy and environmental responsibility.
